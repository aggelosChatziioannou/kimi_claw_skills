# Deep Research - Comprehensive Multi-Source Research

## Purpose
Conduct thorough, multi-source research with real-time progress visibility.

## Why This Skill Exists
Deep research takes 15-20 minutes. Without updates, user thinks I'm stuck. This skill provides transparency throughout the entire process.

## Research Process (With Live Updates)

### Phase 1: Query Analysis (30 sec)
**Status:** 🔬 Initializing research on [topic]...
- Understanding research scope
- Identifying key angles to investigate
- Planning source strategy

### Phase 2: Broad Search (2-3 min)
**Status:** 🌐 Scanning sources...
**Live Updates:**
- "📡 Searching: General overview of [topic]..."
- "📡 Searching: Recent developments in [topic]..."
- "📡 Searching: Expert opinions on [topic]..."

### Phase 3: Deep Dive (5-8 min)
**Status:** 🔍 Examining sources in depth...
**Live Updates:**
- "📄 Reading: [Source 1] - collecting key findings..."
- "📄 Reading: [Source 2] - cross-referencing facts..."
- "📄 Reading: [Source 3] - looking for gaps..."
- "📄 Reading: [Source 4] - verifying claims..."
- "📄 Reading: [Source 5] - gathering statistics..."

### Phase 4: Synthesis (3-5 min)
**Status:** 🧩 Synthesizing information...
**Live Updates:**
- "🔄 Comparing findings across sources..."
- "🔄 Identifying patterns and trends..."
- "🔄 Cross-verifying key facts..."
- "🔄 Organizing into structured report..."

### Phase 5: Report Generation (2-3 min)
**Status:** 📝 Compiling final report...
**Live Updates:**
- "✍️ Writing executive summary..."
- "✍️ Structuring key findings..."
- "✍️ Adding citations and sources..."

---

## Output Format

### Executive Summary
Brief overview of what was found (2-3 paragraphs)

### Key Findings
1. **Finding 1** - with supporting evidence
2. **Finding 2** - with supporting evidence
3. **Finding 3** - with supporting evidence
...

### Trends & Patterns
- Trend 1
- Trend 2
- Trend 3

### Gaps Identified
What information was NOT found (future research directions)

### Sources Consulted
1. [Title] - [URL] - Key point: ...
2. [Title] - [URL] - Key point: ...
...

### Confidence Assessment
- High confidence: Facts verified by 3+ sources
- Medium confidence: Facts from 1-2 sources
- Speculative: Insights requiring further verification

---

## Usage

```
"Κάνε deep research για [θέμα]"
"Deep research: [topic]"
"I need comprehensive research on [topic]"
```

## Expected Duration
- Simple topic: 10-15 minutes
- Complex topic: 15-25 minutes
- Very complex: 25-35 minutes

## Communication Protocol

**MANDATORY:** Status updates every 2-3 minutes during research.

**Never:** Go silent for more than 5 minutes without update.

**Always:** Tell user what source I'm reading right now.
