# Second Brain

**ID:** second-brain  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Author:** Kimi-Claw  
**Compatibility:** OpenClaw, Markdown files

---

## Purpose

AI-powered personal knowledge management system. Captures, organizes, and synthesizes your notes, ideas, and thoughts into a connected knowledge base that grows smarter over time.

---

## What It Does

### 1. **Capture Everything**
- Brain dumps (voice or text)
- Meeting notes
- Ideas and thoughts
- Research snippets
- Links and references

### 2. **AI Organization**
- Auto-categorization
- Smart tagging
- Linking related ideas
- Creating connections

### 3. **Knowledge Synthesis**
- Pattern recognition
- Insight extraction
- Contradiction detection
- Weekly/monthly summaries

### 4. **Daily Insights**
- Morning brief on your knowledge
- Connection suggestions
- Pattern highlights
- Trending themes

## Folder Structure

```
second-brain/

├── areas/           # Ongoing areas of interest
├── resources/       # Reference materials
├── archives/        # Completed/inactive
└── insights/        # AI-generated syntheses
```

## How It Works

### On Capture:
1. Save to `inbox/` with timestamp
2. AI processes content
3. Auto-suggests tags/categories
4. Finds connections to existing notes
5. Moves to appropriate folder

### On Query:
1. Search across all notes
2. Find relevant content
3. Surface connections
4. Synthesize answer
5. Suggest related notes

### On Synthesize:
1. Review recent captures
2. Identify patterns
3. Extract insights
4. Detect contradictions
5. Generate summary
